Zoom Phone turns your Zoom app into a full business phone system. Whether you're making your first call, managing a high call volume on a support team, or staying reachable while traveling, this guide walks you through every feature you need — in the order you need it.

## Who this guide is for

This guide is built for everyone with a Zoom Phone license, including:

- **Individual contributors** making and receiving business calls day-to-day
- **Sales and customer service teams** managing high call volumes
- **Executives and assistants** using delegation features to share call handling
- **Remote workers** staying connected from anywhere

Whether you're setting up your account for the first time or mastering advanced features, this guide covers every step.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Zoom Phone license assigned by your IT admin
- Access to the Zoom desktop app, mobile app, or web portal at zoom.us
- Your activation email from Zoom (for first-time setup)
- A few minutes to configure your emergency address and voicemail

## Common questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Where do I set my emergency address?">
    Start with [Account Setup & Settings](/users/account-setup). Setting your emergency address is required for safety and compliance — it's displayed to first responders when you dial an emergency number.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I record a voicemail greeting?">
    Check out **Voicemail & Greetings** for step-by-step recording instructions. You can record via microphone, upload a file, or use text-to-speech.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I forward calls when I'm out of the office?">
    Yes. See **Call Forwarding & Rules** for temporary and permanent forwarding options, including forwarding to a colleague, external number, or voicemail.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I text from my business number?">
    Visit **Sending & Receiving SMS** to start texting customers and colleagues from your Zoom Phone line.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my assistant needs to answer my calls?">
    Set up **Call Delegation** to have assistants manage your calls seamlessly, including answering, placing, and transferring on your behalf.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
