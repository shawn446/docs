---
title: "Send and Receive SMS from Your Business Number"
description: "Use Zoom Phone to send text messages and images from your business number, manage SMS threads, and message individuals or groups up to 10 recipients."
---

Zoom Phone SMS lets you send and receive text messages directly from your business phone number using the Zoom desktop app, mobile app, or web portal. Conversations sync across all your devices, so you can start a thread on desktop and continue it on mobile without losing context. SMS is useful for appointment reminders, quick confirmations, sending images of documents, and any situation where a text is faster than a call.

<Note>
  SMS must be enabled on your Zoom Phone number by your admin before the SMS tab appears. For US and Canada numbers, 10DLC registration is also required — your admin handles this setup.
</Note>

## What you can send

- Text messages (standard SMS)
- Images — JPG, PNG, and GIF files, one at a time (MMS)
- Group messages to up to 10 phone numbers at once

<Warning>
  International SMS does not support sending images. Text-only messages are supported for international numbers when enabled by your admin.
</Warning>

## Accessing SMS

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Phone tab">
        Launch the Zoom desktop app and click **Phone** in the left sidebar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Click the SMS tab">
        Click the **SMS** tab at the top of the panel to view all your conversations.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Zoom">
        Launch the Zoom mobile app and tap **Phone** at the bottom of the screen.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap SMS">
        Tap the **SMS** tab at the top to access your text message threads.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Web app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Sign in">
        Go to **zoom.us** and sign in. Click **Phone** in the left menu.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open SMS">
        Click the **SMS** tab to view and send messages.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Sending a new SMS message

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Start a new conversation">
        Click the compose icon (pencil or + symbol) below the SMS tab to open the compose window.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter the recipient">
        In the **To** field, type a name or phone number. Search your contacts directory or enter a number manually.

        For multiple recipients, add up to 10 contacts or numbers. If a contact has multiple phone numbers, click the arrow next to their name to choose which one to use.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Compose your message">
        Type your message in the text field. Messages can include text and emojis.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Send">
        Press **Enter** or click the send icon. The message sends from your Zoom Phone number.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Tap compose">
        In the SMS tab, tap the compose icon.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select a recipient">
        Enter a name or phone number in the **To** field. Choose from your contacts or enter a number manually.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Type your message">
        Enter your text in the message field using your keyboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Send">
        Tap the send icon. The message delivers immediately.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Replying to an SMS

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the conversation">
        Click on any existing SMS thread in the SMS tab. The full conversation displays.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Type your reply">
        Click in the message field at the bottom and type your response.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Send">
        Press **Enter** or click the send icon. Your reply appears in the thread.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select the conversation">
        Tap the SMS thread. The full conversation opens.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Reply">
        Tap the message field, type your response, and tap the send icon.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Sending images (MMS)

You can attach one image at a time to a message along with optional text.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open or start a conversation">
        Open an existing thread or start a new message.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Attach an image">
        Click the **File** button. Select a JPG, PNG, or GIF image from your computer.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add text (optional)">
        Enter a message to accompany the image. The text and image appear together.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Send">
        Press **Enter** or click the send icon. The image sends with your message.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open or start a conversation">
        Select an existing thread or start a new message.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add an image">
        Tap the **+** icon, then tap **Photo Album**. Select the image you want to send.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add text (optional)">
        Enter a message if desired.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Send">
        Tap the send icon. The image delivers to the recipient.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  The recipient may see the text and image in separate bubbles depending on their carrier and SMS client. Only one image can be sent per message.
</Note>

## Sending to a group (up to 10 recipients)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new message">
    Click or tap the compose icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add multiple recipients">
    In the **To** field, enter up to 10 names or phone numbers, adding each one individually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose and send">
    Type your message and send it. The message goes to all recipients simultaneously.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Understand how replies work">
    Each recipient replies individually — there is no shared group thread. Each reply creates a separate one-on-one conversation with you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing SMS conversations

**Desktop:** Hover over any message and click the **ellipsis (•••)** icon to access options:
- **Copy** — copies the message text to your clipboard
- **Delete** — removes the message for you only (the recipient still sees it)
- **Save as** — saves an image to your device
- **Open File Location** — shows where an image is stored locally

**Mobile:** Tap and hold any message to see options including **Copy**, **Delete**, and **Save Image**.

<Note>
  When you delete a message, the other participant in the conversation can still see it. Deletion only removes it from your view.
</Note>

### Notification settings

To enable or disable notifications for a specific conversation, open the thread, tap or click the conversation settings, and toggle notifications on or off. You can also configure SMS notifications at the system level through your device's notification settings (Windows, macOS, iOS, or Android) as well as in the Zoom app's notification preferences.

## Common issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't send SMS messages">
    Verify that SMS is enabled for your phone number. Contact your admin if the SMS tab doesn't appear in the app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Recipient not receiving messages">
    Confirm you entered the correct phone number including the country code. Check whether the recipient's carrier blocks certain types of messages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Images won't send">
    Ensure the image is in JPG, PNG, or GIF format. Only one image can be sent at a time. Images are not supported for international SMS.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="SMS conversations not syncing across devices">
    Make sure you're signed into the same Zoom account on all devices and that each device has an active internet connection.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Error: 10DLC registration required">
    Your admin needs to complete 10DLC registration to enable SMS for US and Canada numbers. Contact your IT team.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  All SMS conversations sync automatically across your devices. Start a conversation on your desktop in the morning and pick it back up on your phone without losing any messages.
</Tip>
