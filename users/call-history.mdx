Your call history gives you a complete record of every call made or received through Zoom Phone — answered calls, missed calls, voicemails, and declined calls — across your desktop app, mobile app, and web portal. Use it to return missed calls quickly, verify who you spoke with, review call duration, and access any voicemails a caller left after you didn't pick up.

<Info>
  Estimated time: 5 minutes. Prerequisites: Active Zoom Phone license and Zoom desktop app, mobile app, or web access. Calls must be made or received through Zoom Phone to appear in history.
</Info>

---

## What your call history includes

Every entry in your call history shows:

- Caller name and phone number
- Call direction (inbound or outbound)
- Date and time
- Call duration
- Call status (answered, missed, declined, or voicemail)

## Viewing call history

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Desktop app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Phone tab">
        Launch the Zoom desktop app and click **Phone** in the left sidebar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Access call history">
        Click the **History** tab at the top of the Phone panel. Your complete call log appears.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review call details">
        Each entry shows caller information, time, and duration. Icons indicate the call type (missed, answered, voicemail). Click any entry to see full details.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Navigate to Phone">
        Open the Zoom mobile app and tap **Phone** at the bottom of the screen.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open the History tab">
        Tap **History**. Scroll through your call log or tap any call entry for more details.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Web app">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Access Zoom web">
        Go to **zoom.us**, sign in with your credentials, and click **Phone** in the left navigation menu.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open History">
        Select the **History** tab. Browse your call activity and filter as needed.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Identifying missed calls

Missed calls display with distinct visual indicators so you can quickly identify which calls need follow-up.

**How to spot missed calls:**

- **Red icon or badge** next to the call entry in the list
- **"Missed Call" label** displayed in the call log
- **Push notification** on mobile devices (if notifications are enabled)
- **Desktop notification** if configured in your notification settings
- **Badge count** on the Phone tab showing the number of unread missed calls

**Quick actions available for missed calls:**

- Tap or click the phone number to call back immediately
- View caller details before returning the call
- Check if the caller left a voicemail

## Filtering and searching call history

Narrow your call history to find specific calls quickly.

**Filter by call type:**

- All calls
- Missed calls only
- Voicemails
- Inbound calls
- Outbound calls

**Search by name or number:**

Type a caller's name or phone number in the search bar above your call list. Results filter instantly as you type. Use this to find a specific call without scrolling through your entire history.

**Filter by date range:**

View calls from today, yesterday, the last 7 days, or a custom date range. This is helpful when reviewing call activity for a specific project, billing period, or follow-up window.

## Taking action from call history

### Return a call

Click or tap the phone icon next to any call entry. The call connects using your Zoom Phone line immediately.

### View call details

Click or tap the entry to expand it and see the full caller information, call duration, and exact time. Use this to verify details before a follow-up conversation.

### Access related voicemail

If the caller left a voicemail after a missed call, a voicemail icon appears on the history entry. Tap or click it to listen to the message directly from the call history — no need to navigate to the Voicemail tab separately.

### Add to contacts

Click or tap on the caller's number in the call details, then select **Add to Contacts**. Save the number for future quick access.

## Pro tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Enable notifications for missed calls">
    Turn on desktop and mobile notifications so you never miss an important call. Configure this in **Settings** → **Notifications** in both the Zoom app and your device system settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Review history daily">
    Make it a habit to check your call history each morning to catch any missed calls from the previous evening. A quick scan takes under a minute and ensures no follow-up falls through the cracks.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use search for quick lookups">
    Instead of scrolling through a long list, type the caller's name or number in the search bar. Results appear instantly — much faster than manual scrolling, especially if you have high call volume.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Prioritize missed calls with voicemails">
    When reviewing missed calls, look for the voicemail icon first. A caller who took the time to leave a message deserves a faster callback — they've signaled that their reason for calling is important enough to document.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Common issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Not seeing recent calls in history?">
    Refresh the app or check your internet connection. Call history syncs when you're online — a dropped connection can cause a delay in new entries appearing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Missing calls from specific people?">
    They may have called your personal mobile number instead of your Zoom Phone number. Confirm which number you've shared with them.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Call history not syncing across devices?">
    Ensure you're signed into the same Zoom account on all devices and that each device is connected to the internet. History syncs across devices tied to your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't find older calls?">
    Use the date filter or search function to locate older entries. History is retained but may require filtering or searching if you have a large number of calls. Try expanding the date range in your filter.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Notifications not appearing for missed calls?">
    Check your notification settings in both the Zoom app (**Settings** → **Notifications**) and your device's system settings. Both must allow notifications for missed call alerts to appear.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
