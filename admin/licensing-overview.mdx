Zoom Phone uses a two-part licensing model: every user needs a base license, and most users also need a calling plan that determines where they can dial. Choosing the wrong combination wastes budget or leaves users unable to make external calls. This guide explains every license type, walks you through calculating your costs, and shows you how to optimize your allocation.

<Info>
**Estimated time:** 15 minutes

**Audience:** IT admins, procurement teams, finance and budget managers

**Prerequisites:** Access to Zoom account billing information, your user inventory from the planning guide
</Info>

## License types explained

### Base licenses

Every Zoom Phone user starts with one of three base licenses.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Basic License" icon="user">
    Extension-to-extension dialing only. Includes internal calling, voicemail, and call history. No calling plan required.

    **Best for:** Warehouse staff, contractors, internal-only users

    **Cost:** Included with Zoom Phone purchase
  </Card>
  <Card title="Licensed User" icon="phone">
    Everything in Basic plus outbound calling to external numbers, inbound calls from external numbers, and SMS/MMS messaging (US/Canada).

    **Requires:** A calling plan (see below)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Common Area License" icon="building">
    For shared devices, not individual users. Optimized for walk-up use in conference rooms, lobbies, and reception areas.

    **Cost:** Lower than standard user licenses
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Calling plans

Add a calling plan to a Licensed User base license to enable external calling. Choose the plan that matches each user's actual calling behavior.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="US & Canada Metered">
    - **Cost:** $10/user/month
    - **Includes:** Pay-per-minute for calls
    - **Best for:** Very light calling users (under 100 minutes/month)

    <Warning>
    Costs add up quickly with regular use. Monitor usage monthly and upgrade users who exceed 100 minutes.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="US & Canada Unlimited">
    - **Cost:** $15/user/month
    - **Includes:** Unlimited calling to US & Canada
    - **Best for:** Most standard business users
    - **Most popular option**
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Pro Global Select">
    - **Cost:** $20/user/month
    - **Includes:** Unlimited US & Canada calling plus 40+ countries
    - **Best for:** International teams, sales teams calling overseas
    - **Includes popular countries:** UK, Australia, India, Mexico
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
90% of organizations use US & Canada Unlimited for most users. Only assign Global plans to users who actually call internationally.
</Tip>

## Bundled vs. unbundled licensing

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Unbundled (Phone only)">
    Purchase Zoom Phone licenses separately from Zoom Meetings.

    - Good for organizations that only need phone service
    - Flexibility to mix and match plans per user
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bundled (Zoom Workplace)">
    Combines Zoom Meetings and Phone in one license.

    - **Zoom Workplace Pro Plus:** $18.32/user/month — includes Meetings + Phone Unlimited
    - **Zoom Workplace Business Plus:** $22.49/user/month — adds advanced features
    - **Saves 15–20%** vs. purchasing separately
    - Best for organizations using both Zoom Meetings and Phone

    <Note>
    If 50% or more of your users need both Meetings and Phone, bundled plans save money.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Steps to determine your license needs

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your user inventory">
    Pull your user list from the planning guide and categorize users by calling behavior:

    - **High volume** — sales, support, executives
    - **Moderate** — standard office workers
    - **Minimal** — warehouse, part-time
    - **Internal only** — extensions only
  </Step>

  <Step title="Match users to license types">
    Use this mapping to assign licenses:

    | User Type | Recommended License |
    |-----------|-------------------|
    | Power users (sales, executives, support) | US & Canada Unlimited or Pro Global Select |
    | Standard office workers | US & Canada Unlimited |
    | Light users (warehouse, part-time) | Basic (no calling plan) |
    | International callers | Pro Global Select |
    | Shared spaces | Common Area License |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Calculate your costs">
    Create a cost breakdown before purchasing:

    | License Type | Quantity | Cost per License | Total Cost |
    |-------------|----------|-----------------|------------|
    | US & Canada Unlimited | 150 | $15 | $2,250 |
    | Pro Global Select | 20 | $20 | $400 |
    | Basic (extension only) | 30 | $0 | $0 |
    | Common Area | 10 | $10 | $100 |
    | **Total** | **210** | | **$2,750** |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your current inventory">
    1. Log into the Zoom Admin Portal
    2. Go to **Account Management** > **Billing**
    3. View your purchased licenses
    4. Compare to your needs — do you have enough? Too many?
  </Step>

  <Step title="Plan for growth">
    - Add a 10–15% buffer for new hires
    - Consider seasonal staffing needs
    - Budget for license flexibility
  </Step>
</Steps>

## License optimization tips

**Right-size your licenses.** Don't give everyone the highest tier. Match licenses to actual usage patterns.

**Monitor usage regularly.** Review call analytics monthly. Downgrade users who aren't using premium features.

**Reclaim inactive licenses.** When employees leave, remove their license immediately to reassign it.

**Use Basic licenses strategically.** Internal-only users don't need calling plans. Save $15/user/month.

**Bundle when it makes sense.** If you're buying both Meetings and Phone, bundles save 15–20%.

**Plan for add-ons.** Some features (Power Pack, SMS) may require additional costs. Budget accordingly.
